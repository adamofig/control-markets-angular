## 1. The Power of Hyper-Specialized Personas
Generic AI gives generic advice. To win in a niche, your `AgentNodes` must have depth.

- **Recommendation**: Create a "Chief Technical Officer" agent for technical niches, and a "Creative Director" for lifestyle niches. 
- **Pro Tip**: In the Persona definition, include specific "unpopular opinions" or unique viewpoints related to your niche. This makes the content stand out from the standard LLM output.

## 2. Source Curating vs. Script Writing
Don't write scripts yourself. Use the platform to **transmute** high-quality sources into your voice.

- **Recommendation**: Connect a `WebsiteNode` (Link) or `DocumentNode` (PDF) containing high-value data (e.g., industry reports, competitors' top blog posts).
- **Pro Tip**: Use the **Two-Source Strategy**. Connect one source for *facts* and another (like a viral TikTok transcript) for *structure/style*. Let the AI merge them.

## 3. The "Content Waterfall" Workflow
One of the most effective ways to use Control Markets is to build a waterfall flow:
1.  **Input**: A long-form source (e.g., a 10-minute YouTube video transcript).
2.  **Process (Task 1)**: Break it down into 5 key "hooky" insights.
3.  **Process (Task 2 & 3)**: Turn each insight into a Twitter Thread and a TikTok Script simultaneously.
4.  **Output**: Different nodes for different channels.

## 4. Iterative Multi-Modal Feedback
Don't just generate text. Use the `VideoGenNode` or `ImageGenNode` to see how the story looks visually.

- **Recommendation**: If the video script feels "off," don't edit the script manually. Go back to the **Task Prompt** or the **Agent Persona** and adjust the core logic. This fixes all future outputs, not just the current one.

## 5. Frequency over Perfection
In social commerce and niche markets, the algorithm rewards consistency.

- **Recommendation**: Build a "Template Flow" that you can swap sources into every morning. Spend 5 minutes finding a source, 1 minute running the flow, and 10 minutes polishing the output.
- **Pro Tip**: Use the **TikTok Source Ideas** guide to find fresh input every single day.
