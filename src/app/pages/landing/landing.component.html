What is a persona based agent?

A personified Character is how an agent will act like a person. Human characteristics or a distinctive identity to interact more naturally.

Define a name, a distinctive personality, and a voice with a unique accent for each agent.

Establish a marked communication style, including common phrases and filler words.

Expresses itself in the first person, such as "I think" or "I can help you," and adapts its responses according to the relationship with the user.

They are aware that they are AI with a personality and do not try to pretend to be a real person, maintaining transparency in their nature.
